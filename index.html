About me

Hi! My name is Raymond, I live on the Coast Salish Territories, in Cowichan.

I live in one of 7 listed Traditional Villiages of Cowichan Tribes, located in Coast Salish Territories on Vancouver Island, Duncan BC. Quamichan (Kwa’mutsun) Comiaken (Qw’umiyiqun) Koksilah (Xwulqw’selu) Somena (S’amuna’) Clemclemaluts (Lhumlhumuluts’) Khenipsen (Xinupsum) Cowichan Bay (Tl'lulpalus)
